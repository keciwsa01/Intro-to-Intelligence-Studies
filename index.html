Module 1
Introduction & Development of US Intelligence
Explore the historical foundations of American intelligence from the Revolutionary War through the creation of the CIA.

Key Topics

  • Early American intelligence operations
  • Pearl Harbor and intelligence failures
  • OSS formation and legacy
  • CIA establishment and National Security Act
Module 2
US Intelligence Community
Examine the structure, roles, and relationships within the modern U.S. Intelligence Community.

Key Topics

  • 17 IC agencies and their missions
  • Director of National Intelligence role
  • Inter-agency coordination challenges
  • Budget and resource allocation
Module 3
Intelligence Collection
Discover the various methods and technologies used to gather intelligence from multiple sources.

Key Topics

  • HUMINT: Human intelligence operations
  • SIGINT: Signals intelligence collection
  • GEOINT: Geospatial intelligence analysis
  • OSINT: Open source intelligence
Module 4
Analysis, Counterintelligence & Covert Action
Learn about intelligence analysis techniques, counterintelligence operations, and covert action programs.

Key Topics

  • Analytical tradecraft and methods
  • Cognitive biases in analysis
  • Counterintelligence operations
  • Covert action authorities and oversight
Module 5
Policy Making & Intelligence Oversight
Understand how intelligence informs policy decisions and the mechanisms for democratic oversight.

Key Topics

  • Intelligence-policy relationship
  • Congressional oversight mechanisms
  • Executive branch supervision
  • Judicial review and FISA courts
Module 6
Cyber Intelligence & Transnational Issues
Explore modern challenges including cyber threats, terrorism, and global intelligence cooperation.

Key Topics

  • Cyber threat landscape
  • Digital forensics and attribution
  • Transnational organized crime
  • International intelligence sharing
Module 7
Ethical & Moral Issues in Intelligence
Examine the ethical dilemmas and moral considerations facing intelligence professionals and democratic societies.

Key Topics

  • Privacy vs. security balance
  • Torture and enhanced interrogation
  • Whistleblowing and accountability
  • Democratic values in intelligence work
